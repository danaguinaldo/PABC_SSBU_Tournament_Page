Philippine-American Baptist Church

Super Smash Bros. Ultimate Tournament 2019

Hey Houston Smashers!



Philippine-American Baptist Church is hosting a local tournament to support our youth group to go to Camp Fusion. This tournament is a fundraiser for our youth members to return to a baptist camp called Camp Fusion. Camp Fusion is a baptist youth camp that our members go to every year to learn more about Jesus and experience faith.

There is 128 MAX player limit, but you can register on this page by clicking the register button at the top. Those who sign up are guaranteed a spot if present during the time of sign ups, which is 9:00AM. Registration closes at 9:50AM.

April 6, 2019

April 6, 2019 is when the official tournament will begin at 10:00 AM CST after our short devotional and our officials complete the bracketing process.
