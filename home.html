CALLING ALL HOUSTON SMASHERS!

About

Philippine-American Baptist Church is hosting their first ever Smash Bros. tournament to earn enough money to get transportation for all the youth members to get to Camp Fusion. Camp Fusion is a Baptist camp held every summer for teens, ages 13-18, to experience true faith and enjoy a Christian environment, and be taught by pastors, speakers, and counselors about Jesus Christ. Because of this there will be no cash prize because all funds will be donated to the church, and instead 1st place will receive a trophy prize. Players of all ages are welcome and you may bring your friends to watch as well. Please join us at Philippine-American Baptist Church and have a fun time playing smash with community around us. Hope to see you there!
