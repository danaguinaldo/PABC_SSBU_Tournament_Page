CALLING ALL HOUSTON SMASHERS!

Philippine-American Baptist Church is hosting a local tournament to support our youth group to go to Camp Fusion. Camp Fusion is a baptist youth camp that our members go to every year to learn more about Jesus and experience faith.

About

This tournament is a fundraiser to get enough money for transportation to take our youth members to a baptist camp called Camp Fusion. For that reason there is no cash prize, but rather a trophy prize for 1st place.
